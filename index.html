Welcome To Study Point !


Hello ! My name is Garima Sharma and welcome to my website. Many NCEA students in New Zealand Secondary Schools struggle with coping with immense loads of study, assessments and assignments. During this time such students lack study efficiency and the skill of how to organize the study which you conduct. Therefore which is why I have created this website called Study Point. Which aims to provide resources and information for students who want to improve their study skills and their efficiency in studying their respective subjects as NCEA High School Students.
